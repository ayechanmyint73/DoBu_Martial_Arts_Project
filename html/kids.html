KIDS PROGRAMS

Kids Jiu-Jitsu Class

Our Jiu-Jitsu classes offer a unique blend of traditional martial arts and modern self-defense. Led by expert instructors, you'll embark on a journey of physical and mental growth, mastering the art of Jiu-Jitsu in a welcoming and motivating atmosphere. We focus on:

  • Learning practical self-defense techniques
  • Developing mental toughness, focus, and discipline
  • Building self-esteem, confidence, and overcome challenges
  • Fostering quick thinking and effective decision-making
  • Engaging in a focused and disciplined practice

Kids Judo Class

Discover the world of Kids Judo at our gym, where children build strength, agility, and respect. Our experienced instructors guide young judokas through fun and educational sessions, fostering skills that go beyond the judo. We focus on:

  • Developing physical fitness through judo's dynamic movements
  • Instilling discipline and respect through judo's emphasis on proper etiquette
  • Building self-confidence as kids learn to execute throws and techniques
  • Enhancing coordination and balance through the practice

Kids Karate Class

Here is our Kids Karate program, where kids will embark on a journey of self-discovery and empowerment. Through structured lessons, they'll gain physical prowess, focus, and the confidence to overcome challenges. We focus on:

  • Developing focus and concentration through karate's structured routines
  • Encouraging goal setting as kids work towards achieving different belt levels
  • Equiping kids with practical self-defense skills
  • Fostering overall well-being by combining physical exercise with mental discipline
