About Us

Open since 2010, DoBu Martial Arts community believes in a “safety first” approach to the martial arts, in playful environment that nurtures learning. We always look after each other, building up the newbie from their first day onward. Our experienced instructors foster a supportive environment where members of all ages and skill levels can thrive. Our diversity contributes greatly to our individual and group mastery of the arts.

We look forward to guiding your journey of study along the martial way.
